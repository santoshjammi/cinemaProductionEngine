1. Purpose

This bibliography lists the works, papers, systems, and bodies of theory that informed the design of the Genesis Engine. It is not a tutorial reading list. It is a provenance record for the architectural decisions encoded in the constitutional hierarchy.

The bibliography is versioned: additions require a minor version bump; removals require governance approval because downstream documents may depend on the citation.
