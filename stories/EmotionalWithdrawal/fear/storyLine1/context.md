# Context

## Territory
Married couple's suburban home and surrounding cityscape

## Theme
The erosion of intimacy through unspoken fears and pressures

## Psychological Truth
Intimacy requires vulnerability, and when that becomes too risky, it withers.

## Characters
John (35)
Software Engineer
Reserved, anxious, perfectionistic
A man who once initiated affection but now retreats into silence

Emily (32)
Graphic Designer
Warm, intuitive, increasingly frustrated
A woman who feels growing distance from her husband and seeks understanding

## Relationship
Married for 4 years; no children yet; shared history of a passionate courtship that has cooled under the weight of unspoken expectations

## Setting
Suburban home with modern decor in a quiet neighborhood; present day; an environment that should feel safe but increasingly feels cold and distant

## Emotional Atmosphere
A pervasive sense of loss and confusion; life moves at a slow, heavy pace as both characters struggle to reconnect

## Visual Language
Muted interiors, distance inside shared rooms, night scenes, long pauses, hands almost touching but never quite making contact

## Ending Emotion
A deep sadness mixed with the faint glimmer of hope that understanding can lead to healing
