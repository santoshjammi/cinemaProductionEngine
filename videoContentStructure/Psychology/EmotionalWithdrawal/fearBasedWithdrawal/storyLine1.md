1. Why Some Men Stop Initiating Intimacy After Marriage

Emotional Core

Love became pressure.

Storyline

A newly married man was affectionate, playful, constantly initiating closeness.

Over time:

* work stress increases
* emotional expectations increase
* performance anxiety quietly grows
* one or two moments of rejection stay with him

Now:
he waits for signs of certainty before initiating anything.

Eventually he stops trying entirely.

His wife thinks:

“He doesn’t want me anymore.”

Reality:

intimacy became psychologically unsafe.

Key Scenes

* lying awake beside partner
* pretending to sleep
* almost touching her arm, then stopping
* long showers
* emotional silence after “not tonight”
