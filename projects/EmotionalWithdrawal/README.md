# Emotional Withdrawal — Territory

> **This is the canonical reference for the emotional_withdrawal territory.**

## What this territory is

Cinematic psychological storytelling about **emotionally interrupted relationships** — not broken ones.

The core theme:
> Your niche is not broken relationships. It is emotionally interrupted relationships. That's much deeper.

The strongest moments are not the sad moments. They are **almost moments**:
- almost touching
- almost speaking honestly
- almost reconnecting
- almost vulnerable

Emotional tension lives in **interruption**, not in despair.

## Audience response

- **Act 1**: "I know this feeling."
- **Act 2**: "I didn't realize this was underneath it."
- **Act 3**: "That truth hurts."
